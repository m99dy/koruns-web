Essential Oils

We have been using essential oils in our production for more than 20 years. The essential oils are chosen with due diligence by our specialists to guarantee the best quality.

In response to growing interest for essential oils and essential oil blends as of 2016 our company introduces essential oils to our offer portfolio.

Essential Oils & Essential Oil Blends

Some of the essential oils in our offer: grapefruit oil, bergamot oil, orange oil, lemon oil, peppermint oil, lavender oil, ylang-ylang oil tea tree oil and others available for offer on request.

By blending essential oils we create new aromatic compositions that combine both the beneficial qualities and aesthetic olfactory appeal of constituent oils.

Quality Control

Responsibility for raw material, manufactured product batch and product safety control in accordance to EU directives.

Attendance of EU Safety Assesment of Cosmetics courses by our specialists ensures compliance of our fragrances to the latest cosmetic industry standards. We are certified by EN ISO 9001:2015.

Quality Control and Documentation

In accordance with the industry standards and codes of good practice we provide all the necessary documentation such as, but not limited to Material Safety Data Sheets, Allergen lists, Quality Certificates and IFRA certificates.

Research & Development

Innovations department

Supports creation of exclusive developments with innovative functional characteristics. Carries out scientific research of fragrant compound compositions for the purpose of further application to various product types.

Application department

Carries out fragrance testing and evaluation in over 90 different product bases for evaluation by stability, intensity, substantivity and organoleptic, physical and chemical indicator parameters.

Stability and Compatibility checks

We consider our work to be done well only if it results in creation of a commercially successful product together with you. Before being placed on our offer list, our fragrance creations undergo a series of compatibility and stability tests in various product model bases.

We place great value on individual approach to every client. Should you provide us with your product base, we will perform the necessary tests during offer development stage. Thus you will recieve for evaluation fragrances tested for compatibility with your product base as well as aromatised final product samples, which will help you make the best choice.
